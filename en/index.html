About

least initiates and accompanies artistic, ecological, social and scientific participatory approaches, whose objective is to activate the collective awareness and resilience of our ecosystem, through the sharing of knowledge. Bringing together experts and artists, the projects will be co-created with local groups (associations, schools, residents, etc.).

The programme is divided into four parts: co-creative and inclusive projects, continuing education for young artists, a popular and nomadic school of transition and research residencies in Switzerland and Southern Europe.

Team

Eight people are behind least, its conception, mission, programme and operation.

Together, they decide on calls for projects, develop shared thinking on agreed themes, and accompany the implementation and development of each project. They appoint staff.
